Welcome to MailTrout,

a Google Chrome browser extension that uses language analysis to detect phishing emails.

To use MailTrout to analyse an email, highlight the email text in your web mail client, and select the icon at the top right of your browser window.

Once the MailTrout popup has appeared, press the button to analyse your selected text and predict if your email is legitimate or a phishing attempt.

MailTrout will display the results of its analysis, as well as any suggestions about what you should do next.
